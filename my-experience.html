My Experience

This website was created based on my experience of being a person with a hearing difficulty for a day. Every task I did felt like a challenge; talking to people, speaking up, trying to listen to people talk, asking for help, crossing the road, etc. I was scared to even order a coffee because I could not hear myself. I translated these challenges into mini games, so that people can understand how it feels like to experience hearing difficulties.

Not only the social problems, there were also physical dangers. I could not hear the cars coming from the sides, or warning sound/alerts. This made me feel very conscious to my surroundings, making me nervous.

On the top right corner there are some links that are helpful if you are experiencing hearing difficulties. If you are looking for external resources, the links also direct you to organizations that specialize in hearing impairments, where you can find community support and additional information about adaptive technologies.
